Strategy to Portfolio (S2P)
AI Product Review Analyzer
Business Problem
Small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs), such as online shops on platforms like Kaspi or Wildberries, receive a large number of customer reviews every day.
However, they face several challenges:
•	They do not have enough time to read all reviews manually 
•	It is difficult to identify common problems across many reviews 
•	Decision-making becomes slow and inefficient due to lack of clear insights 
As a result, valuable customer feedback is often misused, which can negatively impact product quality and customer satisfaction.
Proposed Solution
The AI Product Review Analyzer is an AI tool that will automatically process and analyze customer reviews.
The system will:
•	Analyze all reviews at once 
•	Identify overall sentiment (positive, negative, or mixed) 
•	Extract the most common problems mentioned by customers 
•	Highlight key positive aspects of the product 
•	Generate actionable business recommendations 
Business Value
•	Saves time by eliminating the need to manually read all reviews 
•	Improves decision-making with structured insights 
•	Identifies critical issues quickly, helping businesses react faster 
•	Enhances customer satisfaction by addressing real customer concerns 
•	Supports business growth through better product and service improvements
